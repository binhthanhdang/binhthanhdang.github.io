Overview

Hi everyone. People used to call me Joseph Dang, and you're free to call me using the same name.

I have completed my doctoral studies in Computer Science at Victoria University of Wellington, New Zealand, where I successfully defended my thesis in 2023. My research interests primarily lie in the field of intelligent algorithms, with a specific focus on greedy algorithms and metaheuristics. These algorithms will be used to solve optimization problems, particularly in the domain of combinatorial optimization. Additionally, I am actively involved in integrating optimization algorithms with machine learning and artificial intelligence techniques to address challenges in forecasting, classification, and recommendation systems.

I am currently an IEEE, IEEE Computational Intelligence Society, IEEE Young Professionals, and IT Professionals New Zealand (ITP) Member.
